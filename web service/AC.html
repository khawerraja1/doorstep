More About AC Install and Repair Services

Professional AC Installation/AC repair & HVAC Installation/HVAC Repair Services

AC or air conditioning is a necessity during summer. Pakistan suffers from major heat waves from time to time. To survive heat exhaustion, people tend to be using AC out of necessity, be it at home or in the office.

Due, to the frequent usage of Air Condition. Maintenance and service become essential for the extended life of your AC.

Kam Kaj provides an easy solution to all your air conditioner problems by making AC repair and AC installation services available at your doorstep all over Karachi and Lahore.

Kam Kaj offers AC repair service to all models of AC units from small residential-sized to big-scale industrial-sized. There is nothing that we do not know about Air conditioning systems.

Often during cold weather, we tend to ignore our AC services as we hardly use them. However, for it to function efficiently, regular AC services are a must.

If not maintained properly, you might need to replace our air conditioners frequently, which can be heavy on pocket. Some common issues that your AC unit may face are gas leakage, frozen evaporator cells, fan problems, AC not blowing cold air, or gas refilling, etc.

Whether you require an AC repair service or AC installation, Kam Kaj’s highly skilled professionals are just a call away. Our professionals are trained in advanced technologies and they are well seasoned.
